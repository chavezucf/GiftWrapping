Services

Diamond Grinding

Essentially a Wet Sanding. This process removes surface scratches, surface wear and tear marks, etch marks, etc…This will make the floor smooth and ready for polishing

Sealing

Penetrating sealer protects the floor from the inside out, filling the pores with silicone and creating a protective barrier at the floor surface

Polishing

Process gives the floor shine. If the floor was not diamond grinded, it will clean the surface as well

Crystallizing

Crystallizing will give the floor an additional boost of shine and hardens the surface.

Lippage Removal

Removes lippage (difference in height between tiles) between tiles prior to polishing…the ULTIMATE service

Stripping

Removal of topical coatings or wax to expose stone

Repairs

Repair of holes, cracks, loose tiles and more

Maintenance

Service plans to keep your stone looking great

Walls and Countertops

All above services are also available for walls and countertops

Types of Stone we Service

  • Slate
