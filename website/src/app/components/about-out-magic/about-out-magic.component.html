About Our Magic

My name Emily Bonnette this is my North Pole operation! To me Christmas is a time of year that brings people together and there really is nothing that gets me more excited! The people, the presents, the lights, the snow (well not so much anymore but I grew up in Ohio).

Growing up I remember locking myself in my room, blasting Christmas music and spending hours wrapping presents! I had my own little Christmas tree lit up in the corner and all the wrapping paper I could steal from my moms closet. I would always try to “trick” people and wrap my presents in different shapes and sizes! One of my favorite memories is when I got old enough to help my dad! He would sneak all the presents for my mom in my closet and have me wrap them for her! My goal is to bring that service my dad entrusted me with to all of you!
